MIYAZAKI Chair Factory

Miyazaki Chair Factory was founded in 1969 and since then we have continued to develop our craftsmanship.

Back in 2000, in collaboration with mainly the designers Murasawa Kazuteru, Koizumi Makoto and Inoda+Sveje, we strengthened our main philosophy ‘Chairs Nobody Else Can Make’.

The famous Danish designer Kai Kristiansen trusted Miyazaki to take back into production his well-known designs such as the 4110, Handy, the paper Knife Sofa.

We are proud to present this combination of outstanding craftsmanship and design, hoping to bring delight and satisfaction to our customers all over the world.

BAKÉLITE®

Polyoxybenzylmethylenglycolanhydride, or simply: BAKÉLITE®. In the early 1900s, Bakélite®, the first fully synthetic thermoset was reported by Belgian chemist Léo Baekeland. Since its discovery, it has had a myriad of applications including radios, phones, cameras, musical instruments, plugs and switches. Having all but disappeared in the manufacture of consumer goods today, NEW JUMO CONCEPT has nonetheless undertaken the task of preserving this material in its rerelease of the JUMO Classique, in order to stay true to the original’s quality and spirit.

+

The arm

The arm, composed of 2 brass parts, has been reinforced on its 3 rotational axes, in order to ensure the JUMO Classique ’s strength over time.
The switch is now activated on demand, and not by the opening / closing of the lamp.

+

THE BASE

The cable spool under the base of the lamp, which holds the cable by spring with space to insert the plug, demonstrates the evolution of the JUMO Classique.

The Bakélite®

The Bakélite® shell and base, a material with modern appeal, is available in black or cream for this 1st reedition.
The lamp now features a warm LED bulb, ensuring output, long life and low power consumption.

+

The brass

The brass articulated arm, has been carefully realised, with particular care placed on its 3 rotational axes.

+

THE BASE

Only one change to the original model, a switch will allow the JUMO Classique to be turned on and off while remaining in the open position. If desired, space at the base of the arm will you to fold the JUMO Classique leaving the switch untouched and the lamp turned off.
