MIYAZAKI Chair Factory

Miyazaki Chair Factory was founded in 1969 and since then we have continued to develop our craftsmanship.

Back in 2000, in collaboration with mainly the designers Murasawa Kazuteru, Koizumi Makoto and Inoda+Sveje, we strengthened our main philosophy ‘Chairs Nobody Else Can Make’.

The famous Danish designer Kai Kristiansen trusted Miyazaki to take back into production his well-known designs such as the 4110, Handy, the paper Knife Sofa.

We are proud to present this combination of outstanding craftsmanship and design, hoping to bring delight and satisfaction to our customers all over the world.
